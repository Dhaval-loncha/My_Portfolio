About Me

Hi, I'm Dhaval, a student in my final year studying Computer Science and Technology. I really enjoy building stuff for the web, especially the parts that people see and interact with, like buttons and menus. I've been learning how to make websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These are like the building blocks of the internet, and I've been getting pretty good at putting them together to make things look nice and work well. During my time in college, I've worked on some projects where I've practiced what I've learned. Some were simple, like making a personal website, while others were more complex, like building a web app that lets people share photos. I'm always interested in learning more and improving my skills. Whenever I come across something new or challenging, I dive right in and figure it out. I like to keep up with the latest trends and techniques in web development so that I can stay ahead of the game. In the future, I hope to work as a frontend developer, creating awesome websites and applications that people love to use. I'm excited to work in real-world projects. When I'm not studying or coding, you can find me outdoors, exploring nature and enjoying fresh air. I also like to read light novels, mangas, manhwas, etc. Finding a balance between work and play is important to me, and I believe that having diverse experiences helps fuel creativity and inspiration.
